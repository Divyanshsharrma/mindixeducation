Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Mindix Education and our career transformation programs

Courses & Training

What courses does Mindix Education offer?

We offer comprehensive courses in Digital Marketing, Data Science & Analytics, HR Management, Business Development, IT Programming, Graphic Design, and more. All courses include practical training, live projects, and placement support with 95% success rate.

How long are the courses?

Our courses range from 3-6 months depending on the program. We offer both weekend batches for working professionals and weekday batches for students. Flexible timing options available with online and offline modes.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes, we provide industry-recognized certificates upon course completion. Our certificates are valued by top companies and include project work, assessments, and practical skills validation.

What is the course fee structure?

Course fees range from ₹25,000 to ₹50,000 depending on the program. We offer EMI options, student discounts, and early bird discounts. Payment plans available with 0% interest EMI.

Placement & Career

What is your placement success rate?

We have achieved 95% placement success rate with over 5000+ students placed in top companies like Amazon, Flipkart, TCS, Infosys, Wipro, and many startups. Average salary packages range from 3-8 LPA.

Do you guarantee placement?

Yes, we provide 100% placement assistance with guaranteed interview opportunities. While we cannot guarantee jobs, we ensure minimum 10 interview opportunities for every student until they get placed.

Which companies hire your students?

Our students are placed in 100+ companies including MNCs, startups, and local businesses. Top recruiters include Amazon, Flipkart, Byju's, Urban Company, Zomato, TCS, Infosys, HCL, and many more.

What is the average salary after placement?

Average starting salary ranges from 3-8 LPA depending on the course and student's performance. Digital Marketing and Data Science students typically get 4-6 LPA, while IT and Business students get 3-5 LPA.

Support & Guidance

Do you provide career counseling?

Yes, we provide free career counseling with 10+ expert counselors covering IT, Marketing, HR, Business, and other domains. Get personalized guidance on career paths, skill development, and job opportunities.

How do I connect with counselors?

You can connect with our counselors through WhatsApp directly from our website. Simply click on any counselor's "Chat/Call" button to start a free 10-minute consultation session.

What support do you provide after course completion?

We provide lifetime career support including resume building, interview preparation, salary negotiation guidance, and job search assistance. Our alumni network continues to grow and support each other.

Do you provide internship opportunities?

Yes, we provide internship opportunities with our partner companies. Students get hands-on experience during the course, and many internships convert into full-time job offers based on performance.
