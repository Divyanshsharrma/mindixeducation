Refund & Cancellation Policy

At Mindix Education, we aim to provide the best learning experience.

1. Refund Policy

Refund requests are accepted within 3 days of purchase only if course access has not been used.

2. Non-Refundable Cases

  • If course content has been accessed
  • If live sessions have been attended
  • If more than 3 days have passed

3. Cancellation

Users can cancel before accessing the course. After access, cancellation is not applicable.

4. Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be processed within 7–10 business days.
